Project Info:

Chemistri devised and rolled out a simple yet highly effective acquisition and nurture campaign using Kademi for their client Shadforth Financial Group. The campaign targeted individuals at 3 stages (5, 10 and 20 years) prior to their retirement.

The ECRM campaign leveraged features including Data Capture, CRM, Email Automation, Blogs and Calendars for event management.

  • Developers: Chemistri
  • Client: Shadforth
  • Project Type: ECRM Campaign
