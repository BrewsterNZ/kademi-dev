Get started today!

Get instant access to FuseLMS and start hacking files directly by accessing your account via your favourite WebDAV client like Bitkinex, Cyberduck, Netdrive... Simply follow these steps:


1. Create your account

Use the form to the right to get instant access to your own personal FuseLMS account.

2. WebDAV server address

For the server address use the URL from your browser when you log into your dashboard.


3. WebDAV User ID

To get your user ID, from your dashboard go Groups & Users > Users > Edit your profile > Obtain your UserId from the Update your details panel.


4. WebDAV password

The password you used to create your account.

Once connected simply access your files via your mounted drive.


5. Questions or feedback

If you have any questions or feedback we would love to hear from you. Simply hit the green chat button on the right of your browser.
