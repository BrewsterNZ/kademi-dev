Project Info:

Reckitt Benckiser's HealthHub was launched to engage with its pharmacy channels, helping drive recommendations of their OTC drugs at point of sale.

Using a combination of eLearning, rewards and industry news, the online program targets different roles within a pharmacy to deliver a highly customised experience.

  • Client: Reckitt Benckiser
  • Project Type: Channel Engagement
