Kademi's intuitive drag and drop interface will allow you to setup training programs with great ease.

Create beautiful training content

Video made easy!

Whenever you upload a video into a module page we'll automatically encode the video ensuring it works across tablets and mobile devices.

Create quizzes and assessment using Kademi's form builder. Choose from multiple choice radio buttons, drop down or free text fields. Enter help text to prompt your users and easily configure the correct answers.
